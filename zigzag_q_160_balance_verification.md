## Findings

1. **Compute is perfectly balanced** for rows y=2-9 (all ~41.73ms, within 0.01ms variation)
2. **DRAM access causes the imbalance**: Original spread of 63.24ms reduced to 13.83ms when DRAM is removed
3. **FPU utilization varies significantly by row**:
   - Rows y=2-3: ~46-54% (heavily DRAM-bound)
   - Rows y=4-6: ~51-80% (moderately DRAM-bound)
   - Rows y=7-11: ~72-99% (compute-bound)
4. **The ~28ms cores in rows 10-11** have fewer Q chunks by zigzag design (not an imbalance issue)
