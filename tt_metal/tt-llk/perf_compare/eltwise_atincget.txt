========================================================================================================================================================================================================
ATINCGET-gated arm/freeze — first arrives → arms, last arrives → freezes + reads
========================================================================================================================================================================================================

Every thread issues 2 TT_ATINCGET ops (entry + exit) on L1 atomic counters.
Pre-increment value tells thread its arrival order. Branching decides who
does the heavy work. Others fall through.

Goal: keep MMIO writes serialised (only ONE arm + ONE freeze + read), but
inject a small symmetric cost on every thread so the cross-thread metric
does not pick up asymmetric delay.
