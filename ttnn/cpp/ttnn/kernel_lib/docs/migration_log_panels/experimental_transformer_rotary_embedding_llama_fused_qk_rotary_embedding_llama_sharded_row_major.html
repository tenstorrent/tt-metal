Decisions — hover code for tooltip, click a focus button to isolate one decision

  • 1 · include swap — Added eltwise_chain.hpp and eltwise_convenience.hpp; matmul/bcast includes unchanged.
  • 2 · sin multiply → mul<> chain — sin_interim = rotated*sin (single-tile) became mul<rotated_in_interm_cb, sin_cb, sin_interm_cb, BroadcastDim::None, InputLifecycle::DeferredPop, InputLifecycle::CallerManaged, OutputLifecycle::DeferredReserve, …>(1). The mul<> now folds the push/pop: rotated_in_interm_cb is DeferredPop (caller waited; chain pops) and the output is OutputLifecycle::DeferredReserve (caller reserved; chain pushes) — the manual cb_push_back(sin_interm) / cb_pop_front(rotated_in_interm) lines are gone.
  • 6 · cos & add stages blocked — Only the sin stage migrates. The cos multiply and the final add use the runtime-selected in_cb/out_cb (is_q chosen at runtime), but chain template args require constexpr CBs — same blocker as the sibling rotary_embedding_llama_sharded.cpp, compounded by the TRISC2 code-size budget.
