**hypothesis** | 2026-03-10 | stage: affine_transform
- problem: ttnn.tilize() cannot tilize a (1,1,1,W) tensor because H=1 < 32. Tilize requires H to be a multiple of 32.
- fix plan: Pad gamma/beta H dimension to 32 before tilizing. Use ttnn.repeat on dim -2 to replicate the single row 32 times, making shape (1,1,32,W), then tilize. Or reshape to (1,1,32,W) by repeating. Alternative: use torch.repeat on host before sending to device, or use ttnn.pad.
