Why it helps

Correctness-critical protocol

A receiver slot is reusable only after every dependent action completes

1 · reservesender waits for slot credit
2 · receiveFabric payload lands in receiver L1
3 · notifyproduced sequence becomes visible
4 · drainTensix writes persistent DRAM output
5 · consumeBRISC/NCRISC finish the window
6 · creditslot returns to sender
Deadlock fixes
Separate initialization and completion epochs; independent forward/backward produced sequences; grouped credits; exact wraparound ordering.
Performance trap
A fused payload+atomic originally waited synchronously for terminal local-NoC completion before the router could progress.
Safe pytest + tt-triage were used for every hang; no --dev build
