 **Zeroth Law of Thermodynamics:**
   - **Statement:** If two systems are in thermal equilibrium with a third system, then they are in thermal equilibrium with each other.
   - **Significance:** This law provides a basis for the concept of temperature and allows the use of thermometers to measure temperature.
