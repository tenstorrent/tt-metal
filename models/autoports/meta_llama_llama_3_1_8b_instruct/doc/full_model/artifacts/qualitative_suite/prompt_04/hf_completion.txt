The translation of "Hello, how are you today?" to French is:

"Bonjour, comment allez-vous aujourd'hui?"

However, a more common and informal way to say it in French would be:

"Salut, comment ça va?"

"Bonjour" is a more formal greeting, while "Salut" is more casual and used with friends or people you know well.<|eot_id|>
