The translation of "Hello, how are you today?" to French is:

"Bonjour, comment allez-vous aujourd'hui?"

Here's a breakdown:

- "Bonjour" means "hello" or "good day"
- "comment" means "how"
- "allez-vous" is a polite way to ask "how are you"
- "aujourd'hui" means "today"

However, a more common way to greet someone in French is:

"Bonjour, comment ça va?"
