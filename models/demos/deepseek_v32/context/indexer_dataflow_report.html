Lightning Indexer — dataflow

Math

Queries (64 heads, from the MLA latent):

$$ q = \mathrm{view}_{B,S,H_i,d_i}\!\big(W_{qb}\, qr\big),\qquad q \leftarrow \big[\mathrm{RoPE}(q_{pe})\ \|\ q_{nope}\big] \in \mathbb{R}^{B\times S\times 64\times 128} $$

Key (single head, shared by all 64 query heads):

$$ k = \big[\mathrm{RoPE}(k_{pe})\ \|\ k_{nope}\big],\quad k_{pe},k_{nope}=\mathrm{split}\,\mathrm{LN}(W_k x) \in \mathbb{R}^{B\times S\times 128} $$

Per-head gate — four factors folded into one multiplier before the kernel:

$$ w_{b,s,h} = \underbrace{\big(W_p\, x\big)_{h}}_{\text{learned gate}} \cdot \underbrace{H_i^{-1/2}}_{=64^{-1/2}} \cdot \underbrace{s_q[b,s,h]}_{\text{q dequant}} \cdot \underbrace{d_i^{-1/2}}_{=128^{-1/2}} $$

Score — heads are summed out; one scalar per (query, key):

$$ I[b,s,t] = \Big(\sum_{h=1}^{64} \mathrm{relu}\big(\langle q_{b,s,h},\, k_{b,t}\rangle\big)\, w_{b,s,h}\Big)\, s_k[b,t] \in \mathbb{R}^{B\times S\times T} $$

Selection:

$$ \mathrm{topk\_indices} = \mathrm{arg\,top}_{\kappa}\big(I + M_{\text{causal}}\big),\qquad \kappa=\min(2048, T) $$
Hadamard is orthogonal — \(\langle Hq, Hk\rangle = \langle q,k\rangle\) — and exists only so fp8 quantization stays accurate; with fp8 dropped (CPU/TT functional port) Hadamard, act_quant, \(s_q\), \(s_k\) all reduce to identity. MLA then converts the indices to an additive \(\{0,-\infty\}\) mask over the key axis (shared by all 128 MLA heads).
