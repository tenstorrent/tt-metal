Anatomy of a single step

Each step runs cond and uncond concurrently on two 2×8 submeshes via a ThreadPoolExecutor(max_workers=2). On the critical thread the time splits between device trunk replay, host PyTorch, and the host↔device transfers. Composition measured on the 720×1072×81 smoke shape (step wall 3046 ms, post device-proj_out); proportions carry to the 189-frame production step.

Dual-expert layer & its collectives

Every layer carries two disjoint weight sets — an und (text) expert and a gen (diffusion) expert — that interact only inside a two-way joint softmax. Each RowParallel projection reduce-scatters then all-gathers back, so every TP linear is an AllReduce in disguise: 4 RS + 4 AG per layer.

Ranked levers

By recoverable wall-clock, hardest-to-cheapest. The device trunk owns 63% of the step but its two biggest costs are structural; the cheap host wins are largely spent.

1

CCL collectives — 4 RS + 4 AG per layer

Every RowParallel linear reduce-scatters then all-gathers to re-replicate the residual. Collapsible only by keeping the residual TP-fractured to trunk exit — the reverted d9558a9 / be0347 / ec524f2 experiment. Multi-day, needs PCC root-cause.

structural
2

BinaryNg 29% — cut call count, not kernel

1.3 ms × 6688 dispatch-bound calls: residual adds (4/block), gelu_decomposed binaries per Linear, k-mask add. Fusion/elimination wins here. gelu_decomposed is load-bearing for LoRA — risky to touch.

fusion
3

Ring-joint SDPA 24% — shape-bound

Mean 8.8 ms × 819 calls; grew from 2.3% at 128² to 24% at 720p×81f. Kernel-tuning lever (HiFi4 + fp32 accum is forced — bf16 accum NaNs at this scale), not call-count.

kernel
4

Host proj_in → device (Phase B)

~100 ms/step still on CPU. Ported version shrank uploads 6× but landed at PCC 0.83 (ttnn.multiply broadcast suspected). Reverted, gated behind a flag for debug.

blocked · PCC
5

VAE decode 63s = 8.3× H200

Conv3d “no known best blocking” fallback on the 10×10 grid. t_chunk=1 already the sweep winner; needs a conv3d blocking heuristic tuned for these shapes.

open
6

sp=2 vs NVIDIA cp=4

Per-step gap tracks the SP factor. sp_factor=4 produces noise on full 4×8 BH Galaxy, so production falls back to dual 2×8 submeshes at sp=2 — a ~2× trunk ceiling if the bisect lands.
